Japanese Garden

The Japanese Garden on La Trobe Campus is a peaceful retreat that offers visitors the opportunity to experience traditional Japanese garden design.

The garden features a koi pond, a tea house, and several walking paths that wind through lush greenery and over arched bridges. It's the perfect place to escape the hustle and bustle of campus life and find some tranquility.

Bird Walk

The Bird Walk is a must-visit spot for any nature lover. Located just south of the Library, this walk is the perfect place to spot a variety of bird species that call the La Trobe Campus home.

With a walking path that winds through wooded areas and around a small lake, the Bird Walk offers visitors the chance to get up close and personal with a variety of bird species. Bring your camera and capture some amazing shots of these beautiful creatures.

Campus Green

The Campus Green is a central gathering place for students, staff, and visitors. With plenty of open space for picnics, games, and outdoor events, it's the perfect place to soak up some sun and enjoy the great outdoors.

Throughout the year, the Campus Green hosts a variety of events and activities, including concerts, movie nights, and food festivals. Keep an eye on the campus calendar so you don't miss out on any of the fun!
